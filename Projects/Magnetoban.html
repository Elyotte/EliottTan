Magnetoban - Sokoban

A Sokoban-style puzzle game developed in a team of five at ISART Digital between January and April 2023.

Each team member designed two levels, and three additional levels were generated by ChatGPT as part of a research project on Artificial Intelligence in game design.

You play as Roboban, a small robot tasked with organizing metal crates in a warehouse.
