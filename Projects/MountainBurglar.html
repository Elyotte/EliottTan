Mountain Burglar - Endless runner

Developpment time : 7 days


Made in one week for the Brackeys Game Jam. Play as a marmot on the run from a biscuit factory, collect biscuits to boost your cart’s speed and escape!

game's page

- Implemented a fully functional one-directional character.

- Integrated the Proton Scatter plugin into Godot 4, accelerating the game artists integration pipeline.

- Focused on enhancing the game feel by designing a system where the music intensity scales with the player’s speed.
