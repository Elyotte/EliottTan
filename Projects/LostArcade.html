Lost Arcade - Shoot'Em up

Play as a spirit and face off against a ghost in a haunted arcade room. Use your ability to slow down time for a short moment to navigate your way toward the ghost.

- Implemented a time-slowing mechanic.

- Designed enemy patterns and levels.

- Handled audio mixing through code, dynamically adjusting sound pitch during slow motion.

- Created boss fights.

- Designed 2D particles.

- Implemented localization through code, without using Godot’s built-in translation tool.

- Created graphical assets using Godot’s Polygon2D node.
